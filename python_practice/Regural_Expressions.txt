Regular Expression Pattern 

. > match any single occurance
\. > match itself
* > match zero or more occurance of previous character
+ > match one or more occurance of previous character
? > match one or more occurance of previous character
{} > match min and max occurance of previous character
[] > match any of the number
\s > match a space, tab or newline
\d > match a digit
\w > match  a number, digit or -
\b > boundry match
[^ ] > match none of occurance in character class
^ > start of string
$ > end of string
